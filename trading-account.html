FAQ

Account questions, answered

What is a forex trading account?
A forex trading account is an account held with a regulated broker that lets you buy and sell currency pairs — and other CFDs. You deposit funds, log in to a platform such as MT4 or MT5, and trade on the price movements of instruments like EUR/USD without owning the underlying asset.
Which account is best for forex trading?
It depends on your style. Newcomers often start with the Standard account (commission-free with tight spreads), while active and high-volume traders prefer ECN or Prime ECN for raw spreads from 0.0 pips and a small per-lot commission. If you're unsure, try a free demo first.
How do I open a forex trading account?
Register in a few minutes, complete a quick identity verification (KYC), fund your account from as little as $50, and start trading. You can open a live account or practise first with a free demo.
What types of forex trading accounts are available?
STARTRADER offers a free Demo account plus three live accounts — Standard, ECN and Prime ECN — each with different spread and commission structures to match your experience level and trading volume.
What is the minimum deposit required for a forex trading account?
You can start with a deposit from just $50. A larger balance simply gives you more flexibility with position sizing and risk management — it isn't required to begin.
How do I choose a forex broker?
Look for strong regulation, competitive spreads and commissions, fast and reliable execution, secure funding options, quality platforms (MT4/MT5) and responsive support. STARTRADER is regulated across multiple jurisdictions and covers all of these.
What are the fees associated with a forex trading account?
Your main cost is the spread, plus a small per-lot commission on ECN and Prime ECN accounts. The Standard account is commission-free (except indices). Positions held overnight may incur a swap fee, and there are no deposit fees.
Can I open a forex trading account with multiple brokers?
Yes. Many traders diversify across brokers. With STARTRADER you can also open several accounts under one profile — for example a Standard and an ECN account, or a live and a demo account side by side.
How do I manage risk in my forex trading account?
Use stop-loss and take-profit orders, size your positions sensibly, avoid over-leveraging, and never risk more than you can afford to lose. Every STARTRADER account also includes negative-balance protection so you can't lose more than your deposit.
What are the benefits of using a demo account before opening a live forex account?
A demo account lets you practise with virtual funds in real market conditions — test strategies, get comfortable with the platform and build confidence with zero financial risk before you trade live.
