FAQ

Application questions, answered

What documents do I need for the application?
You'll need a valid photo ID (passport, national ID or driver's license) and a proof of address — such as a utility bill or bank statement — dated within the last three months.
What are the requirements for these documents?
Documents must be valid and in date, show all four corners and full details, match the information you registered with, and be issued by a recognised authority or provider.
What if I don't have any of the specified documents?
No problem — our customer support team is available 24/7 to guide you through the alternatives that may be accepted for your region.
Are other documents accepted in special cases?
Yes. In certain cases we can accept additional documents such as a birth certificate or citizenship certificate. Reach out to support to confirm what applies to you.
Are there restrictions on document quality?
Documents must be clear and in full colour. Scanned, blurry, black-and-white, cropped or damaged files cannot be accepted.
What happens if there's an issue with my application?
If anything needs attention, we'll email you directly with a clear explanation of the next steps so you can complete your application quickly.
